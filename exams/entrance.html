Entrance Exam Preparation Tips

1 Understand the Syllabus

Carefully analyze the official syllabus for your target exam. Make a list of all topics and prioritize based on weightage and your strengths.

2 Create a Study Plan

Develop a realistic timetable allocating time for each subject. Include regular revision cycles and practice tests in your schedule.

3 Practice Previous Papers

Solve at least 5 years' question papers to understand the exam pattern, difficulty level, and frequently asked questions.

4 Take Mock Tests

Regularly attempt full-length mock tests under exam conditions to improve speed, accuracy, and time management skills.
