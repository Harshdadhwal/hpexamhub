Frequently Asked Questions

1. What's the exam pattern for BSc 3rd Year Physics?

Each paper has 50 marks theory (3 hours) + 50 marks internal assessment (practical/viva).

2. Which topics are most important in Quantum Mechanics?

Schrödinger Equation (1D problems), Angular Momentum, and Perturbation Theory are frequently asked.

3. How to prepare for Nuclear Physics practicals?

Focus on GM Counter experiments, Half-life determination, and absorption studies (alpha/beta/gamma).
