‚úàÔ∏è The Journey Begins

Bir Billing, nestled in the Kangra district of Himachal Pradesh, is renowned as one of the best paragliding destinations in the world. As someone who lives for adventure sports, this place was calling my name! The journey itself was mesmerizing - winding mountain roads, lush green valleys, and the crisp mountain air filling my lungs with excitement.

Located at an altitude of 8,000 feet, Billing serves as the takeoff site, while Bir at 5,000 feet is the landing point. The 14 km stretch between them offers one of the most thrilling paragliding experiences in Asia.

This was THE moment I'd been waiting for! After a safety briefing from my experienced pilot, we were ready for takeoff. The initial run-up had my heart racing, and then... lift-off! The ground disappeared beneath my feet, and suddenly I was soaring like a bird. The panoramic views of the Dhauladhar ranges, the patchwork of green fields below, and the rush of wind - it was absolutely surreal. The 20-30 minute flight felt both eternal and too short. Pure, unfiltered joy!

üèïÔ∏è Camping Under the Stars

As a camping enthusiast, I couldn't miss the opportunity to spend nights under Bir's starlit sky. The camping sites here are perfect - surrounded by pine forests with stunning mountain views. I set up my tent near the landing site, and as night fell, the sky transformed into a celestial canvas. The Milky Way was clearly visible, shooting stars made wishes, and the cool mountain breeze made for perfect sleeping weather. Waking up to mountain sunrise with birds chirping was the best alarm clock ever!
