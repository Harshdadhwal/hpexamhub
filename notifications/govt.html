Frequently Asked Questions

Most HP government jobs are applied through the official websites of HPSSSB (hpsssb.hp.gov.in) for non-gazetted posts or HPPSC (hppsc.hp.gov.in) for gazetted officer posts. You need to register, fill the application form, upload documents and pay fees online.

The general age limit is 18-45 years for most posts, with relaxations for reserved categories (5 years for SC/ST/OBC, 10 years for PwD). Some technical posts may have different age criteria, so always check the specific notification.

Focus on: 1) General Knowledge of HP (history, geography, culture) 2) General Studies 3) Quantitative Aptitude 4) English/Hindi 5) Computer Knowledge. Solve previous year papers and take mock tests regularly. Our Previous Papers section has resources to help.

Typically you need: 1) Scanned photograph & signature 2) Educational certificates 3) Caste certificate (if applicable) 4) Domicile certificate 5) Experience certificates (if required) 6) Any other documents specified in the notification.
