How to Improve English for Bank Exams

Reading Comprehension

Regular reading of newspapers, magazines, and quality literature helps improve vocabulary and comprehension skills. Focus on editorials from The Hindu or Economic Times for exam-specific preparation.

Grammar Fundamentals

Master the basics of English grammar including tenses, articles, prepositions, and sentence structure. Practice with Wren & Martin or other standard grammar books.

Vocabulary Building

Learn 10-15 new words daily with their meanings, synonyms, antonyms, and usage. Maintain a personal dictionary and revise regularly.

Practice Tests

Solve previous year question papers and take mock tests to understand the exam pattern and identify weak areas.
