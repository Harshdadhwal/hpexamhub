Important Information - MSc Chemistry HPU

Exam Pattern

  • • Each paper: 70 marks (Theory) + 30 marks (Internal Assessment)
  • • Duration: 3 hours for each theory paper
  • • Practical exams: 50 marks each semester
  • • Viva-voce: 20 marks each semester

Important Topics

  • • Inorganic: Crystal field theory, Metal complexes
  • • Organic: Stereochemistry, Reaction mechanisms
  • • Physical: Quantum mechanics, Statistical mechanics
  • • Mathematics: Calculus, Linear algebra applications
