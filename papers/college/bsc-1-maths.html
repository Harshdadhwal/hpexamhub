Frequently Asked Questions

1. What's the passing marks for BSc 1st Year Math?

Minimum 36% marks (24 out of 60 in theory + 16 out of 40 in internal assessment).

2. Which book is best for Differential Calculus?

"Differential Calculus" by Gorakh Prasad (recommended by HP University).

3. How many questions appear from each unit?

Typically 2 questions per unit (8 total), attempt any 5 questions.
