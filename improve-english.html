Cloze Test 1

In the following passage, some words have been omitted. Read the passage carefully and select the most appropriate word from the given options.

The Internet has (1) ______ revolutionized the way we communicate. It has made the world a (2) ______ place where information can be (3) ______ instantly. However, this convenience comes (4) ______ certain risks that we must be (5) ______ of.

1. (a) completely (b) partial (c) hardly (d) seldom

2. (a) smaller (b) larger (c) wider (d) narrower

3. (a) shared (b) hidden (c) lost (d) forgotten

4. (a) with (b) without (c) for (d) to

5. (a) careful (b) aware (c) sure (d) certain

Cloze Test 2

Fill in the blanks with the most appropriate words from the options given below:

The importance of reading (1) ______ be overstated. It is through reading that we (2) ______ knowledge, expand our vocabulary, and (3) ______ our imagination. Good readers tend to (4) ______ better writers as they are (5) ______ to various writing styles.

1. (a) can (b) cannot (c) should (d) must

2. (a) acquire (b) lose (c) forget (d) ignore

3. (a) limit (b) expand (c) reduce (d) contract

4. (a) become (b) avoid (c) hate (d) refuse

5. (a) closed (b) limited (c) exposed (d) restricted
