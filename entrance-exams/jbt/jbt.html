HP JBT Exam Pattern

Paper Structure

  • 1

    Total Marks: 100

    Objective type multiple-choice questions

  • 2

    Duration: 2 Hours

    120 minutes to complete the paper

  • 3

    Negative Marking: 0.25

    1/4 mark deducted for each wrong answer

Section-wise Distribution

A General Knowledge (20 Questions)

Current Affairs, HP GK, Indian History, Geography

B Teaching Aptitude (20 Questions)

Child Development, Pedagogy, Teaching Methods

C Subject Knowledge (60 Questions)

Language, Mathematics, Environmental Studies

HP JBT Detailed Syllabus

Section A: General Knowledge

  • Current Affairs (National & International)
  • Himachal Pradesh GK
  • Indian History
  • Indian Geography
  • Indian Polity
  • General Science
  • Sports
  • Books and Authors

Section B: Teaching Aptitude

  • Child Development and Pedagogy
  • Learning Theories
  • Teaching Methods
  • Classroom Management
  • Educational Psychology
  • Evaluation Systems
  • Inclusive Education

Section C: Subject Knowledge

  • Hindi/English Language
  • Mathematics (Basic)
  • Environmental Studies
  • Social Studies
  • Science (Elementary Level)
