Our Story

HP Exam Hub was started by a group of students preparing for various government exams in Himachal Pradesh.

We realized how difficult it was to find quality study materials and previous year papers in one place, especially for those who couldn't afford expensive coaching or study materials.

What began as a small effort to share resources among friends has now grown into a platform serving thousands of aspirants across the state.

Our mission is simple - to provide free, accessible exam preparation content to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.
