HP JBT 2024 Detailed Syllabus

हिंदी (25 प्रश्न)

  • वर्ण विचार (स्वर, व्यंजन, संयुक्त व्यंजन)
  • शब्द विचार (संज्ञा, सर्वनाम, विशेषण, क्रिया)
  • वाक्य विचार (वाक्य रचना, वाक्य भेद)
  • शब्द भंडार (पर्यायवाची, विलोम, अनेकार्थी शब्द)
  • संधि (स्वर संधि, व्यंजन संधि, विसर्ग संधि)
  • समास (तत्पुरुष, द्विगु, द्वंद्व, बहुव्रीहि)
  • क्रिया (काल, वाच्य, वृत्ति)
  • कारक (संबंध कारक, करण कारक)
  • मुहावरे एवं लोकोक्तियाँ
  • वर्तनी एवं वाक्य शुद्धि

English (25 Questions)

  • Parts of Speech (Noun, Pronoun, Verb, Adjective)
  • Tenses (Present, Past, Future)
  • Articles & Determiners
  • Prepositions & Conjunctions
  • Modals (Can, Could, May, Might)
  • Direct & Indirect Speech
  • Active & Passive Voice
  • Sentence Correction
  • Synonyms & Antonyms
  • Commonly Misspelled Words

Numerical Ability (25 Questions)

  • Number System (LCM, HCF, Fractions)
  • Percentage & Ratio-Proportion
  • Profit & Loss, Simple & Compound Interest
  • Time, Speed & Distance
  • Time & Work Problems
  • Mensuration (Area, Volume)
  • Algebra Basics
  • Geometry (Lines, Angles, Triangles)
  • Data Interpretation (Tables, Graphs)
  • Averages & Mixtures

General Awareness (25 Questions)

Himachal GK

  • राज्य का इतिहास एवं संस्कृति
  • भूगोल (नदियाँ, पर्वत, जलवायु)
  • प्रमुख मंदिर एवं पर्यटन स्थल
  • हिमाचल के प्रथम (प्रथम मुख्यमंत्री, राज्यपाल)
  • मेले एवं त्यौहार
  • जनगणना 2011 के प्रमुख आँकड़े
  • हाल की महत्वपूर्ण घटनाएँ

National/International

  • Current Affairs (Last 6 Months)
  • Indian Constitution
  • Important Days & Dates
  • Books & Authors
  • Sports & Awards
  • UN & Other Organizations
  • Science & Technology

HP JBT 2024 Preparation Strategy

Section-wise Preparation Approach

Hindi: Focus on grammar rules, practice संधि-विच्छेद daily, and learn 5 new मुहावरे everyday.
English: Build vocabulary through newspaper reading, practice grammar exercises.
Maths: Solve 10-15 problems daily from each topic, focus on speed.
GK: Read HP GK books, follow current affairs monthly magazines.

Time Management Strategy

Divide your 2 hours as:
First 30 mins: Attempt all GK questions (easiest section)
Next 45 mins: Solve Numerical Ability (most scoring if prepared)
Next 30 mins: English section
Last 15 mins: Hindi section + review marked questions

Important Resources

For HP GK: "Himachal Pradesh Ek Parichay" by Ravi
For Hindi: "Samanya Hindi" by Arihant
For English: Wren & Martin Grammar Book
For Maths: Quicker Maths by M Tyra
Mock Tests: Solve at least 10 full-length papers before exam

HP JBT 2024 - Frequently Asked Questions

Q 1. What is the minimum qualifying marks for HP JBT?

For general category candidates, the expected cutoff for government colleges is 70-75 marks while for private colleges it's around 45-65 marks. Reserved category candidates get relaxation as per government norms.

Q 2. How many attempts are allowed for HP JBT exam?

There is no limit on number of attempts for HP JBT exam as long as you meet the age criteria (minimum 18 years) and educational qualification (10+2 with 50% marks).

Q 3. What is the salary after completing JBT course?

After getting government job as JBT teacher, the starting salary is approximately ₹25,000-30,000 per month (Level-5 as per 7th Pay Commission) which increases with experience and promotions.

Q 4. Is there any interview after written exam?

No, the selection is purely based on written exam merit. There is no interview process for HP JBT admission.
